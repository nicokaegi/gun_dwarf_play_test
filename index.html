GunDwarf
Controls
  • Left Click : Shoot and kill
  • Right Click : Mine blocks when near
  • Space : Pause Menu
  • Movement : WASD or Arrow Keys
Objectives
  • Mine as much gold as you can
  • Try not to die :)
GunDwarf
Story

You are a dwarf disgraced and banished by your clan. To reclaime your houner you must venture into the UnderDark, find riches, and bring glory to the ancestors!
